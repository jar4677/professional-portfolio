My Background

It has been a long road which has led me to this point in my varied career. I've spent time as an Architectural Drafter, Retail Manager, Pastor, and most recently a Systems/Data Analyst. Through all of these experiences I have learned a great deal about myself, what kind of work I enjoy, and in which environments I thrive.

In my time as a Systems/Data Analyst I was exposed to software development and had the opportunity to engage in some programming myself. I discovered that the days I spent writing a complex macro were my favorite days. When I found out that there were intensive, hands-on programs where I could learn programming technologies, I knew that was the path for me.

My Career Goals

After quite a bit of reflection and soul searching I have come to a conclusion about the kind of career I want and it is relatively simple.

I want to work in a collaborative, positive environment, with people who are passionate about what they do, and together we make things that help others be successful.
